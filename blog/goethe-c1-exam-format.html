Goethe C1 Exam Format: Complete Guide

June 10, 2026·Goethe C1

Key takeaways

  • Goethe C1 certifies CEFR C1 (advanced).
  • Four skills: Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking — 100 points total.
  • Typical duration: ~3 h 10 min written + ~16 min speaking.
  • This guide lives on c1.goetheprep.com — the C1 prep site.

Preparing for Goethe C1? This guide on c1.goetheprep.com explains the official C1 structure. University study in German, advanced workplaces, and teaching pathways.

What the C1 exam tests

  • Lesen: complex academic and literary texts
  • Hören: lectures and detailed interviews
  • Schreiben: structured argument (~200+ words)
  • Sprechen: sustained monologue + debate

Scoring

Each skill is worth 25 points (100 total). You generally need ≥60 overall with minimums in Writing and Sprechen. See our passing score guide.
