Frequently asked questions

What is the Goethe-Zertifikat C1?

The Goethe-Zertifikat C1 certifies advanced German proficiency. It proves you can understand demanding texts, express yourself fluently and spontaneously, and produce well-structured academic writing. It's the standard requirement for studying at German universities, many advanced professional roles, and teaching positions.

How long is the Goethe C1 exam?

The written portion takes approximately 3 hours and 10 minutes: reading (70 min), listening (40 min), and writing (70 min). The speaking exam adds about 16 minutes, conducted in pairs. Total time is around 3.5 hours.

What score do I need to pass?

You need at least 60 out of 100 points. Each module (Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, Sprechen) must be passed independently. You can retake failed modules without repeating the entire exam.

How does the AI writing review work?

Submit your academic text or essay and receive instant, detailed feedback. The AI evaluates content quality, argumentation, textual coherence, grammatical accuracy and range, and vocabulary sophistication — all calibrated to C1 expectations.
